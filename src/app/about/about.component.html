About Us

Your Local Neighborhood Pharmacy

In order to deliver the most advanced preventative, diagnostic, and therapeutic health care services under one roof at a reasonable cost, Shree Akhshaya Hospital is a fully-fledged multispecialty setup that utilises the most current technology and equipment in their respective categories. Medical professionals, including physicians, nurses, and paramedics, are available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

Our hospital features a full-fledged facility for major and minor surgeries, as well as a 24-hour emergency department, an ICU, a NICU, a labour room, an in-house pharmacy, a fully computerised pathology laboratory, and a digital computerised X-ray machine. At our facility, you can get all of the health services you need.

Health care services provided by Shree Akhshaya Hospital are based on patient and future needs in order to provide the best possible treatment. Before a patient is admitted or discharged from the hospital, a thorough clinical history is taken and the patient is closely monitored.

One thing is certain when it comes to choosing a hospital: quality matters. By offering high-quality medical treatment in a friendly and convenient setting for more than two decades, we help thousands of patients every day feel better about their health and well-being.
